FAQ

Questions we get asked most

Can't find your answer? Chat with us on WhatsApp — we respond instantly.

What exactly is an Apple ID / iCloud lock?

Activation Lock is a security feature Apple introduced with iOS 7. When Find My iPhone is enabled, the Apple ID is linked to the device at Apple's server level. If you buy a second-hand iPhone or forget credentials, the device is completely unusable until the lock is removed.

Will this unlock survive factory resets and iOS updates?

Yes. Our unlock is performed at Apple's activation server level — not a software workaround or jailbreak. Once removed, the lock is permanently gone regardless of factory resets, iOS version changes, or re-activations. It will not return.

Do I need to send my iPhone to you?

Absolutely not. The entire process is done remotely. We only need your IMEI number (dial *#06# to find it) and the locked Apple ID email address. Your device stays with you throughout.

How does the split payment work?

We ask for 50% of the total service fee upfront to begin processing. Once we successfully unlock your device and you confirm access, the remaining 50% is due. If we cannot complete the unlock, your down payment is fully refunded.

How long does the unlock take?

Most unlocks are completed within 2–4 hours of receiving your request and down payment. In rare cases it may take up to 24 hours. We'll keep you updated on WhatsApp throughout the process.

Is this service legal?

Our service is intended for legitimate owners who lost access to Apple ID credentials, purchased second-hand locked devices, or received devices as gifts. We do not assist with stolen devices. By submitting a request, you confirm rightful ownership.

What information do you need from me?

We need: (1) your iPhone model, (2) your 15-digit IMEI (dial *#06#), (3) the Apple ID email on the locked screen, (4) your contact email, and (5) your name. No passwords, no shipping, no physical access needed.

What's your refund policy?

If we are unable to unlock your device for any reason, you receive a 100% refund of your down payment. No questions asked, no delays. In the rare event of failure, we notify you promptly and process the refund immediately.
